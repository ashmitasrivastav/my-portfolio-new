About Me

Hello! I’m Ashmita Srivastava, currently in my final year of a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program. With a deep passion for technology, I've honed my skills in Java programming, full-stack development, and web design. My academic journey has provided me with a solid foundation in creating dynamic and interactive web applications. I specialize in developing both front-end and back-end solutions, utilizing modern technologies to build efficient and scalable systems. Whether it's crafting a sleek user interface or managing complex server-side logic, I thrive on challenges that push my boundaries. In addition to my technical skills, I am a creative problem-solver who enjoys transforming ideas into functional designs. I am enthusiastic about continuous learning and eager to apply my knowledge in a professional setting. My goal is to contribute to innovative projects and grow as a versatile developer in the tech industry.
